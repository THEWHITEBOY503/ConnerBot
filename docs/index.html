What does ConnerBot do?

Lets go over some cool things ConnerBot can do.

Leaderboard

Ever wanted to see who is the most active in your server? ConnerBot has your back. Every time you send a message, ConnerBot adds XP to your account. If you get enough XP, you may get a role!*

Moderation

Someone in your server acting up? Need to ban or kick them? ConnerBot makes that relatively easy! All you have to do is run the command for banning and tag the user!**

Weather

Wanna see the weather around where ConnerBot is hosted?*** You sure can! ConnerBot lets you see a forecast of the hosting location of the bot anytime, updated every 30 min! With this info, you can predict weather or not the power at the location of ConnerBot hosting might get knocked out, or if you live near where ConnerBot is hosted, it can be your weather app!

Fun facts

Do you want to learn something you might not of known? ConnerBot can tell you an intresting**** fact about something!

Open source

ConnerBot is completely open source! Want to make your own version of ConnerBot? Feel free to fork us on GitHub!

*Roles may only be given out only if a server admin tells the bot to do so. XP is not sold.

**Proper permissions are needed in order to kick or ban users. Some normal user in your server can't just kick someone. You're welcome.

***I know not every ConnerBot user lives in Plano. To get weather for other places, make a clone of ConnerBot and change the code so it gets weather for your desired location.;

****Fun Fact may or may not be intresting depending on the user.
